Sound

Frequencies and vibrations are at the basis of the Universe, making Sound an ideal medium for Healing and Self exploring.

I discovered the healing potential of sound and intuitive voice while holding rhythmical sacred space for babies, young children and their parents. My aim at the time as a Steiner teacher was to receive and nurture the young generations holistically and consciously, bathing them into pure, loving frequencies. I then realised how much soothing we get by humming intentionally to ourselves, and how much it resonates like ancient memory of who we used to be, felt from a place of wholeness and wellness.
