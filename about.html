Analucia Feracci is a Sound practitioner, Visual artist and Workshop facilitator. Her works focuses on creating immersive sound environments that induce grounding, heart opening and transformative inner journeys. The intention is to promote relaxation as gateway to awaken cellular memory, harmonise and balance the energetic body and enhance our flow.

That Practice is rooted in Shamanic Energy and Plant Spirit Medecine, using Greenbreath Work, the heart intelligence of flowers and trees, as meditative tool to authentically connect us to Nature.

By choosing Creative Expression as a way to explore Self and deepen Ancestral Wisdom, bridging fields to strengthen connections, there is a possibility of going back to our true essence of origin.

Analucia is trained in Sound Therapy, Visual Art and Vibrational Medecine.
